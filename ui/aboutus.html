What is California Roleplay?

California Roleplay is a FiveM community that provides realistic Roleplay to our players. We expect all players to role-play, meaning to stay in character with whatever persona you choose to play with. We know there is a lot to cover in the information below but please read through this in its entirety. There is a lot of good information that can be found in these FAQs. California Roleplay is not a GTA Online server.

VDM AND RDM IS EXTREMELY PROHIBITED WITHIN OUR SERVER.

Random Death Match (RDM) refers to going up to another player and killing them with no intended roleplay or reasoning behind it. Vehicle Death Match (VDM) refers to taking your car and using it with the intent to kill without a purpose/reason. Do not ram into other vehicles for no reason.
