Creating a Character: When loading into our server, it is required for role-play that you are walking around in a uniquely customized ped rather than the default one given upon connecting into the server. To set this up all you need to do is the following:
Step 1: Hit F1 and click "player related options" then go to "MP Ped Customization"
Step 2: Depending on your perfered gender of play, you would either Create a Male or Female Ped
Step 3: Please select your outfit using your arrow keys and when done click "save" at the bottom then give your player a name to save.
Once saved you can go back and load this outfit at any time by going back to the F1 Menu, selecting "player related options" -> "MP Ped Customization" -> "Saved Character"
