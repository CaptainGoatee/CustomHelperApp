Y - When pressing Y on your keyboard, this will open a menu specific for your character which will give you the option to take off any piece of clothing, jewelry, vests, or any piece of clothing that is attached to your FiveM Character

M - This menu serves a few different purposes for Civilians and Law Enforcement within our community, this menu can be used at any time within the server as long as it's used with the intent to follow rules and abide roleplay and not break server rules.

T - This is how you chat in the game and use various chat commands in our server. We will cover various chat commands below that will help in your role play experience here at California Roleplay.

L - This hides the chat window. If you are in the middle of RP and you want to hide chat, press L. Pressing L again will un-hide the chat window while playing in our servers.

X - Holding X will put your character's hands up.

U - This will cause you to fall over. Press U again to stand back up.

CTRL - To crouch down, press CTRL on your keyboard.

SPACE BAR - This will make your character jump.
