California Roleplay
In-Game Terminology

VDM - Vehicle Deathmatch: killing someone with a car with no intent to roleplay. Bannable offense.

RDM - Random Deathmatch: killing someone with a weapon with no intent to roleplay. Bannable offense.

FRP - Fail Roleplay: performing an action that would not be possible IRL.

NITRP - No Intent to Roleplay: No Intent to Roleplay. Typically seen in a ban message. Don't be a shitter. Do proper RP please!
