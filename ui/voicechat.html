Using Voice Chat

What is Voice Chat?
How do I make that work?

In-game, press ESC and head over to Settings. Head down to Voice Chat. Make sure Voice Chat is Enabled. You can adjust your input (Microphone) and output (Headset or Speakers) in these settings. Most players turn on Push to Talk. Make sure this setting is turned on. The default hotkey is holding down [N] to talk. You can change this in Advanced Keybind Settings to any hotkey you like. Most of us link in-game talk to a mouse button.

Press [Z] on your keyboard to change your talk distance. This is how far players can hear you. Options are: Whisper, Normal, and Shouting. You will see that located in the bottom right-hand corner of your screen next to [VC].
