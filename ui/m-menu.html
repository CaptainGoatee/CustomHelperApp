Civilian Options - M Menu: The actions menu gives players access to Ziptie/Un-Ziptie and Blindfold/Un-Blindfold players nearby. This also gives access for players to conduct a "Hands on Head", "Surrender", and "Get Frisked" emote that is used to increase roleplay when in specific situations. At the bottom of this menu it also gives you the option to play / cancel emotes at any time.

Vehicle Options - M Menu: This tab gives players access to "Cruise Control" and custom Headlight options for their vehicle.

Teleports - M Menu: This tab gives players access to common teleport locations within our server to allow for fast travel if you do not wish to drive from one location to another.
