California Roleplay
In-Game Terminology

AOP - Area of Play: Located in the HUD area on the bottom left hand corner of your screen. As a player, you must stay in the area of play. Los Angeles is way down south, in the middle is Sandy Shores and all the way up North is Paleto Bay. If AOP is statewide you can be anywhere in the map.

WDIF? - What do I find: used in roleplay scenarios with law enforcement officers when searching vehicles or persons. LEO may do a /me WDIF? after searching your person or car. You must respond with a /me.

POSTAL: Located in the HUD on the bottom left of your screen. This indicates the area you are located on the map. You can use this in RP scenarios and to meet up with friends.
