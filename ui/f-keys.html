F1 - VMenu has 2 key sections: Character Customization, which allows you to customize your character's appearance, experiment with different settings, and save your character to set it as your default when joining servers. Vehicle Customization enables you to customize your cars, save them, and use spawn codes from premium car packs in Vehicle Spawner.

F3 - This is your emote menu. You can find a lot of neat emotes your character can do to increase the RP experience when playing. All of these emotes can also be run in chat using /e [emote-name]. Take a look around and pressing on any of those emotes will cause your character to perform that action. Have fun!

F6 - When pressing F6 you will open a custom tailored menu specifically for California Roleplay that will display information regarding the following menus: Free Vehicles, Whitelisted Vehicles, Whitelisted Peds, Donator Packs.
