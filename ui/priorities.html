California Roleplay
In-Game Terminology

Priority Status: Listed in the HUD on the bottom left of your screen. HOLD indicates law enforcement is busy and no police chases or high priority crimes should occur. If a time is mentioned here and Priority is on Cooldown, this indicates law enforcement is taking a break from high priority calls. You must wait for the cooldown to end before starting one.

Peacetime: All murder and violence should cease immediately when peace time is active. Only passive RP is allowed when peacetime is active.

POSTAL: Located in the HUD on the bottom left of your screen. This indicates the area you are located on the map. You can use this in RP scenarios and to meet up with friends.
