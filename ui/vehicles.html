Spawning Vehicles & Vehicle Customization: When spawning in vehicles, you must go through the following process:
Step 1: Press F1 and navigate to "Vehicle Related Options"
Step 2a: If spawning through spawncode, go to Vehicle Spawner and go to "Spawn Vehicle by Model Name" then type in the spawncode, and press ENTER
Step 2b: f spawning a saved vehicle, go to "Saved Vehicles" and then go to the vehicle class that your car is listed under in our server

When customizing vehicles, you must go through the following process:
Step 1: Once in a vehicle, navigate to "Vehicle Related Options"
Step 2: Depending on the modification you would either select Vehicle Colors, Mod Menu, Vehicle Extras, or Vehicle Neon Kits and then customize those options to your liking
